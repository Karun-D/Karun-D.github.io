About Me



Hey!

I'm Karun, a second-year Computer Science student at The University of Guelph, originally from Brampton, Ontario.

I enjoy learning new languages/technologies, reading The Witcher by Andrzej Sapkowski, and watching the greatest club in the world play (Manchester United #GGMU).

My goal is to apply these technologies in a meaningful way by digitally improving the lives of others. I strive to maintain this aspiration in my projects wherever possible, and I intend to make a career out of it.
